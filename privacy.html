Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2025  |  Applies to course2learn.in

Course2Learn ("we", "us", or "our") operates the website course2learn.in. This page tells you about our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of information when you visit our site.

1. Information We Collect

We do not require you to register or create an account to use Course2Learn. We may collect the following non-personal information automatically:

  • Browser type and version
  • Operating system
  • Pages visited and time spent on each page
  • Referring URLs
  • Approximate geographic region (country or city level)

This data is collected through standard server logs and analytics tools (such as Google Analytics) and is used solely to improve the site.

2. Cookies

We use cookies and similar tracking technologies to improve your experience. Cookies are small data files placed on your device. You can instruct your browser to refuse all cookies or to indicate when a cookie is being sent. If you do not accept cookies, some parts of our site may not function properly.

3. Affiliate Disclosure

Course2Learn participates in affiliate marketing programs. When you click a "Visit Course" or "Enroll Now" button and make a purchase on a third-party platform (such as Udemy, Coursera, or others), we may receive a commission at no extra cost to you. This helps us keep the site free and running.

Affiliate links are identified by our redirect domain (course2learn.in/go/...). We only recommend courses we believe are genuinely valuable.

4. Third-Party Links and Platforms

Our website contains links to third-party websites including Udemy, Coursera, YouTube, Google, LinkedIn Learning, and others. Once you leave our site, we have no control over those platforms. We encourage you to review the privacy policies of any third-party sites you visit.

5. Google Analytics

We use Google Analytics to understand how visitors use the site. Google Analytics collects anonymous usage data. You can opt out of Google Analytics tracking by installing the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on.

6. Children's Privacy

Course2Learn is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will take steps to remove it.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated date. We encourage you to review this page periodically.
